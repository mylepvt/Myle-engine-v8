Dashboard — your home

Everything for your day in one place

When you open the dashboard, your priorities and activity are summarized at a glance.

  • Today's priority card — Shows your report status at the top. If you have not submitted today, you will see a reminder.

  • Follow-ups — A list of leads due for follow-up today (retarget leads) appears automatically.

  • Quick actions — Add Lead, Claim Leads, and Submit Report shortcuts are available here.

  • KPI cards — Total leads, revenue, close rate, and wallet balance in four cards.

  • Status breakdown chart — Donut chart showing how many leads are in each stage.

  • Recent leads — Your five most recent leads in a compact card view.

Tip: On mobile, analytics starts collapsed — tap Show Analytics to expand it.

My Leads

Manage your leads

Only leads assigned to you appear here. Admin-owned leads are separate.

  • Open My Leads from the sidebar.

  • Use the search bar to find a lead by name or phone.

  • Use the status dropdown to update status — one click; changes save automatically.

  • Click a lead name to open Edit Lead, where you can update notes, follow-up date, and more.

  • Use filter chips (New, Contacted, Converted, and so on) to narrow the list quickly.

Best practice: Keep status current and add a short note after each touchpoint so admins can follow progress.

Add Lead

How to add a new lead

  • Open Add Lead from the sidebar or top bar.

  • Name and phone number are required. Use a valid 10-digit phone number.

  • Source — Where did the lead come from? (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, referral, and so on.)

  • Status — Start with New Lead.

  • Notes — Add context from the first conversation.

  • Submit. The lead appears in your list immediately.

Duplicate leads with the same phone number are blocked by the system.

Lead pool — claim leads

Purchase leads with your wallet

Admins load leads into the pool; you claim them and they move into My Leads.

  • Open Lead Pool from the sidebar.

  • You will see how many leads are available and the price per lead.

  • In Claim leads, enter how many leads you want.

  • Review the total cost and confirm.

  • The amount is deducted from your wallet and the leads appear under My Leads.

If the pool is low, ask an admin to add more leads — and keep your wallet funded for claims.

Submit your daily report

Send today's KPIs

  • Open Daily Report from the sidebar.

  • Enter today's calls, meetings, and presentations.

  • Optional: add context in Notes.

  • Submit. Admins can see your report on their side.

Submit every day. Members who skip reports may appear as "missing" on the admin dashboard.

Retarget List

Leads due for follow-up

Retarget lists leads you need to contact today or on a set future date.

  • On Edit Lead, set a follow-up date and keep status Retarget where appropriate.

  • Open Retarget list from the sidebar.

  • Today's due leads appear first — use the WhatsApp icon to reach out quickly.

  • After you call or message, update the lead status or set a new follow-up date.

Today's retarget leads also surface on the dashboard "Today's priority" card.

My Wallet

Balance and top-ups

  • Open My Wallet from the sidebar.

  • See current balance, total recharged, and total spent.

  • To top up, scan the admin's UPI QR from this page and complete payment.

  • After payment, copy the UTR from your bank or UPI app.

  • Paste it into Request recharge and submit.

  • Once an admin approves, your balance is credited.

Enter the correct UTR — admins verify each request. Invalid entries are rejected.

Leaderboard

Team rankings

The leaderboard shows everyone's performance with rank order.

  • Open Leaderboard from the sidebar.

  • The table includes total leads, converted, revenue, seat holds, and fully converted counts.

  • Your row is highlighted so you can see your rank at a glance.

Rankings refresh daily — more conversions generally mean a higher rank.

Notice Board

Announcements from admin

  • Open Notice board from the sidebar.

  • All admin announcements appear here — newest first.

  • Read important notices for targets, meetings, and policy updates.

Live Session

Training / Team calls

  • Open Live session from the sidebar.

  • If a session is scheduled, you will see the link and time here.

  • Tap Join to open Zoom or Google Meet.

Training and team calls are often hosted here — join on time when invited.

Profile & Settings

Photo, password, and profile info

  • Click your name at the bottom of the sidebar to open Profile.

  • Upload a profile photo — use a clear, well-lit headshot where possible.

  • Update your full name, phone, and upline name.

  • Use the Password link in the sidebar footer to change your password.

Admin Dashboard

Whole-team overview

  • KPI Cards — Total Leads, Revenue, Close Rate, Pool Count, Wallet Requests, Missing Reports, Seat Hold, Fully Converted.

All Leads

Manage leads for the entire team

  • Open All leads from the sidebar to see every team lead.

  • Filter by member (assignee), status, or search.

  • Change status from the row dropdown without opening the full form.

  • Click a lead name to open Edit lead, where you can reassign to another member.

  • To remove a lead, use Delete lead on the edit page — it moves to the recycle bin first.

Lead Pool Management

Load and manage pool inventory

  • Open Lead pool (admin view) from the sidebar.

  • CSV import — Upload Meta/Facebook lead exports; phone and name columns are parsed automatically.

  • Single lead — Add leads to the pool one at a time when needed.

  • Set lead price — members claim at this per-lead rate.

  • Use the trash icon to remove a lead from the pool.

Keep CSV files under 5 MB. Include Phone and Name columns.

User Approvals

Approve new members

  • Open Approvals — the orange badge shows pending count.

  • Each row shows name, FBO ID, and registration time.

  • Approve — user can log in and join the team workspace.

  • Reject — access is denied; you can add an internal note with the reason.

  • Delete — permanently removes pending or rejected users (not recoverable).

  • Reset password — use when a member forgot their password.

With notifications enabled, you may get a push when someone registers.

Editing a lead — step by step

How to keep records current

  • From any lead list, click the name to open Edit lead.

  • Change status from the dropdown, then save.

  • Notes — add a short recap after calls or meetings.

  • Follow-up date — sets the next touch; the lead surfaces on retarget lists.

  • Use the WhatsApp shortcut to message the lead directly.

  • In Track & advanced payments, pick the track after interview and enter seat-hold amounts.

  • Seat hold received moves the lead to Seat hold confirmed when appropriate.

  • Final payment received marks Fully converted and clears pending balance.

Wallet — end-to-end flow

From top-up to claiming leads

The wallet works in three simple steps:

💳
Step 1
Pay via UPI
📤
Step 2
Submit UTR in the app
✅
Step 3
Admin approves → balance credits

  • On the wallet page, scan the admin UPI QR with Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or your bank app.

  • Enter the amount and complete payment.

  • After success, copy the UTR / reference number (often about 12 characters).

  • Return to the app → Request recharge → paste UTR and amount → submit.

  • An admin is notified, verifies the transfer, and approves.

  • Once approved, your wallet balance updates.

  • You can then open Lead pool and claim leads.

Use the exact UTR from your payment — wrong references are rejected and slow down processing.

Where do I find my UTR?

Copy it from your payment app

  • Google Pay — Payment history → transaction → copy Transaction ID.

  • PhonePe — History → transaction details → UPI transaction ID.

  • Paytm — Passbook → transaction → UPI reference number.

  • Bank transfer — Net banking or SMS often label it UTR or Ref No.

UTRs are often ~12 characters and may be numeric or alphanumeric (for example 406912345678 or KKBK2026XXXXX).
Frequently Asked Questions
How much wallet balance do I need to claim from the lead pool?
Required balance = (number of leads) × (price per lead). Example: 5 leads × ₹50 = ₹250. The Lead Pool screen shows the current price and available count.
If I close the app, is my lead status still saved?
Yes. Status changes write to the server immediately. Closing the tab does not roll them back. The list view saves status via AJAX when you change the dropdown — no separate form submit.
I forgot my password — what should I do?
Use Forgot password on the login screen, or ask an admin to reset it from the Approvals area.
How do I add the app to my home screen (iPhone / Android)?
iPhone: Open the app in Safari → Share (square with arrow) → Add to Home Screen → Add.

Android: Open in Chrome → menu (⋮) → Add to Home Screen or Install app.

You will get a home-screen icon that opens in a full-screen, app-like window.
How do I turn on notifications?
Tap the bell in the top bar → allow browser notifications when prompted. New announcements can trigger push alerts.
I deleted a lead by mistake — can I get it back?
Ask an admin. The sidebar Recycle bin holds deleted leads until they are restored or purged. Members cannot restore on their own.
What happens if I try to add a duplicate lead?
The system blocks a second lead with the same phone number. You will see Phone number already exists. Find the original record and update it instead.
How do I enable dark mode?
Tap the moon icon 🌙 in the top bar to toggle dark mode on or off.
What is the difference between seat hold and fully converted?
Seat hold confirmed — The prospect paid a deposit or token (for example ₹2,000 on Slow Track). The seat is reserved but full payment may still be due.

Fully converted — Full program payment is received; the person is formally onboarded as a member.
I submitted a wallet recharge but it has not been approved yet — what should I do?
Message your admin on the channel you normally use. They also get an in-app signal. Correct UTRs are usually approved quickly. Check request status on the wallet page.
